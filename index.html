What we do?

Azlaan consultants is one of the most innovative architecture and integrated design practices in the East African region. Over the past 2 decades the practice has pioneered a sustainable approach to architecture through a strikingly wide range of work, from urban master plans, Educational, civic and cultural buildings, offices and workplaces to private houses and wide range of Hospitality Projects.

    Vision & Design Philosophy

Azlaan Designs has over the years endeavored to design buildings that ascribe to value creation, both aesthetically and functionality. Our vision for a new building is that it should always add new and positive qualities to the site and the brief. This happens through careful ana­lysis of the site, the surrounding buildings and the brief. We have an approach where we always see the project’s complexity and challenges as a positive creative force to create something unique for the site and purpose. This has resulted in an impressive portfolio of projects and attested by the clients trust in the firms experience and capability.
